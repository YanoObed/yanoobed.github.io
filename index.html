About Me

A bit about who I am and what I do

I'm Obadia — a data professional who enjoys solving messy, real-world data problems that most people avoid. I build systems that stay reliable even when data becomes complex.

I work across CRMs like Salesforce and HubSpot, health systems like DHIS2, and field tools like KoBoToolbox, ODK, CommCare, Ona, and SurveyCTO. I use Python and Google Apps Script for automation and QGIS for spatial data.

On the development side, I use Django, React, and TypeScript to build tools that power Power BI and Tableau dashboards used for real decision-making.
