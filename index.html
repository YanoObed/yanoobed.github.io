A bit about who I am and what I do

I'm Obadia — a data professional who enjoys solving the kind of messy, real-world data problems that most people would rather avoid. I've spent years working with organizations to build systems that actually hold up when the data gets complicated.

A lot of my work involves connecting the dots between different platforms — CRMs like Salesforce and HubSpot, health systems like DHIS2, and field data tools like KoBoToolbox, ODK, CommCare, Ona, and SurveyCTO. I use Python and Google Apps Script to automate the tedious parts, and QGIS when the data has a geographic dimension.

On the development side, I work with Django, React, and TypeScript to build web tools that bring data to life — clean, reliable information that powers Power BI and Tableau dashboards people actually open every day.
